Sports Centre Membership

Join Brunel University Sports Centre and enjoy exclusive benefits and access to our state-of-the-art facilities.

Membership Benefits

  • Free unlimited access to the university gym
  • Free bike rentals for up to 3 hours per day
  • Free sports equipment rental (subject to availability)
  • 25% discount on all fitness classes
  • Priority booking for sports facilities
  • Monthly fitness assessment with our expert trainers
  • Access to exclusive members-only events

Membership Fees

Students: £120 per academic year or £45 per term

Staff: £180 per year or £60 per term

Alumni: £220 per year or £75 per term
