Sports Programs at Brunel

At Brunel University, we offer a wide range of sports programs to cater to all interests and skill levels. Whether you're looking to try something new, stay fit, or compete at the highest levels, we have something for you!

Badminton

Our state-of-the-art courts provide the perfect environment for both casual play and competitive matches. With coaching available at all levels, badminton is one of our most popular sports.

Practice Days: Monday, Wednesday, Friday

Team Selection: Trials held in October each year

Join Now

Basketball

Our basketball program caters to players of all abilities. With men's and women's teams competing in university leagues, we offer professional coaching and regular practice sessions.

Practice Days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

Team Selection: Ongoing assessment throughout the year

Join Now

Swimming

Our Olympic-sized swimming pool is home to our swimming program. With lanes dedicated to different abilities, everyone from beginners to competitive swimmers can come and join us.

Practice Days: Daily sessions available

Team Selection: Time trials held quarterly

Join Now

Football

Our football program is one of the largest at Brunel. With multiple teams competing at different levels, there's a place for everyone who wants to play the beautiful game.

Practice Days: Monday, Wednesday, Saturday

Team Selection: Pre-season trials in September

Join Now

Tennis

Our tennis courts are available year-round, with indoor facilities for when the weather doesn't cooperate. Regular coaching sessions and competitive matches are organized throughout the year.

Practice Days: Tuesday, Thursday, Sunday

Team Selection: Based on ranking matches held monthly

Join Now

Athletics

Our athletics program covers everything from track events to field disciplines. With professional coaching and excellent facilities, we've produced several national champions.

Practice Days: Monday, Wednesday, Friday

Team Selection: Based on performance at university trials

Student Success Stories

Emma Thompson - National Swimming Champion

"When I joined Brunel, I was a good swimmer but lacked the technical refinement needed to compete at the highest level. The coaching I received at the Brunel Sports Centre transformed my technique and approach to training. The flexible scheduling allowed me to balance my academic studies with my athletic pursuits, and the state-of-the-art facilities gave me everything I needed to excel. I'm proud to say that I've now represented both Brunel and my country in international competitions."

James Chen - University Basketball Captain

"The basketball program at Brunel completely changed my university experience. Coming from a school where basketball wasn't a major sport, I found a community of players and coaches who were passionate about developing my skills. The regular practice sessions, competitive matches, and fitness support have helped me become a more complete player. Being selected as team captain in my final year has been the highlight of my time at Brunel."

Aisha Patel - Athletics Scholarship Recipient

"The athletics program at Brunel provided me with the perfect environment to develop as both a student and an athlete. The coaching staff understood the challenges of balancing academic demands with athletic training, and they created personalized programs that allowed me to excel in both areas. The indoor and outdoor facilities meant that I could train year-round, regardless of the weather. Thanks to the support I received, I secured a sports scholarship that has helped fund my education."
